Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Introduction

Insteppe Studio ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide when using our website and services.

Information Collection

Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone)
  • Company and job title information
  • Project details and requirements
  • Correspondence and project-related documents

Technical Information

Automatically collected information may include:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Cookies and session data
  • Information about pages visited

Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide web and mobile development services
  • Manage projects and client communications
  • Improve the quality of our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send important project notifications

Data Protection

We implement modern technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, access control, and regular security system updates. Access to personal data is limited to employees who need it to perform their job duties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or transfer your personal data to third parties, except when necessary to provide services (e.g., hosting providers) or when required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for the time necessary to achieve the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. After project completion, data may be archived for up to 3 years for support purposes.

Your Rights

Under applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict data processing
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect site functionality.

Policy Changes

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes via email or by posting a notice on our website.
